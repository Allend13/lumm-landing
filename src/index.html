Lumm is a web3.0 social app with NFT Collectibles Exchange (MVP) reinventing the interactions between fans and influencers.

NFT is a cool breakthrough technology that allows you to confirm someone's authorship in the digital world. But there is a problem - few people understand how it works and why it is needed.

In order to land this technology, we thought that we need to combine web3.0, the culture of collecting something, and game mechanics.

Users can be both, content creators and buyers. They can mint NFTs, collect, exchange, and contest each other with cards depending on their level.

Content creators or influencers are able to mint tokens by uploading content and selling it at an auction in several clicks. Every person who buys someone’s NFT gets on the list of top fans.
